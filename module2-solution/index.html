OUR MENU

Chicken

Our tender grilled chicken is marinated in a blend of herbs and spices, served with a side of garlic butter rice and fresh garden salad. A hearty and wholesome choice for all ages.

Beef

Enjoy our slow-cooked beef roast glazed in a savory pepper sauce. Served with creamy mashed potatoes and sautéed seasonal vegetables. A true comfort dish for meat lovers.

Sushi

Freshly prepared sushi rolls with the finest ingredients—tuna, salmon, avocado, and cucumber—served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger. A light and elegant meal for refined tastes.
